The Bit Pad
About

Welcome to The Bit Pad, where you can see brand-new stand-up performed by great comics through the best medium for telling jokes: the written word.

How to see a show at The Bit Pad:

  1. Click "I'm Ready to See a Set."
  2. Click on the comic you want to see perform a written show.
  3. Read the comic's material.
  4. Give the comic feedback in order to keep the show going.
  5. Enjoy the show! Or don't.
