The Bit Pad
About

The Bit Pad was inspired by a comedy dilemma.

What do you do when your taste in comedy is limited to just a few comics?
What do you do when you want to hear new material?
What do you do when you don't think other comics are funny?

The answer?

Use technology to generate new material on behalf of your favorite comics.
The medium stinks, but maybe you'll get the humor you're looking for.

Thanks for visiting The Bit Pad. I'm pretty sure you'll think it's tolerable.
